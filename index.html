Why one more tracking app?

Most people who take supplements don't know what conflicts with what, when to take them, or whether the timing matters. Generic food-logging apps treat supplements as an afterthought.

BeastApp is built for people who want one app instead of four: supplements, food, and training in the same place, with the interaction logic that the others skip.
