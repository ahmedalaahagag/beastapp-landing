Cochrane Systematic Reviews

Tier: Authoritative (non-profit)

What it is

Cochrane is an international non-profit that produces the most rigorous systematic reviews in medicine, using the GRADE framework to grade certainty of evidence.

Why we trust it

Reviews are prospectively registered, peer-reviewed, and updated. Cochrane's methodology sets the global gold standard for evidence synthesis.

What we use it for

Evidence synthesis for high-stakes interactions (warfarin + K2, serotonin risk). We defer to Cochrane when it has spoken on a given question.
