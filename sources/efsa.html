European Food Safety Authority

Tier: Regulatory (EU)

What it is

EFSA is the European Union's regulator for food and nutrition. It sets legally binding Tolerable Upper Intake Levels (ULs) for vitamins and minerals sold in the EU.

Why we trust it

EFSA's Scientific Panels conduct systematic reviews and publish transparent opinions before setting ULs. Their UL values become law across the 27 member states.

What we use it for

Primary source for Upper Limits — especially for EU/EEA users. We rank EFSA first in our arbitration when it conflicts with other sources.
