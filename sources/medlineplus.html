MedlinePlus

Tier: Authoritative (U.S. government, consumer-facing)

What it is

MedlinePlus, maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine, publishes consumer-friendly summaries of drugs, herbs, and supplements.

Why we trust it

NLM writers work with clinicians and information specialists to translate peer-reviewed evidence into plain language. Updates are reviewed before publication.

What we use it for

Fallback source for drug-supplement interactions when DrugBank and DailyMed don't cover a case.
